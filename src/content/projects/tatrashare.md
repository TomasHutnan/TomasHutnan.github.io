TatraShare was built for HackKosice 2026 as an AI-powered platform that simplifies shared finances and real-time expense management.

It enables seamless bill splitting, shared savings goals, pooled funds, and instant expense settlement through virtual payment cards integrated with Apple Pay and Google Pay.

The platform also includes AI-driven receipt parsing, smart transaction categorization, and contextual financial assistance.
